Star Wars Species

How awesome would Star Wars be without its different species?

To the right are a list of 37 different Star Wars Species with information about each one.

There are several different types of Star Wars sentient specie lifeforms:

Mammalian Species: Hummans, Wookies, Cerean, Bothans, Sullustans

Reptilian Species: Rodians, Barabels, Chistori, Trandoshans

Plant Species: Neti, Ergesh, Revwiens, Zelosians, Sylphe

Insect Species: Verpine, Fefze, Killik, Bartokk, Gand, Geonosians

Hard Invertebrates: Sauvax, Aganof, Tarc

Soft Invertebrates: Iyra, Hutt, Ee

Avian Species: Vor, Mrlssi, Quor'sav, Rishii, S'kytri, Tikiarri

Aquatic Species: Selkath, Nautolan, Quarren, Herglic, Vurk, Gungan

Exotic Species: Bith, Celegians, Filar-Nitzan, Pulra

Each specie on the right is a sentient.

*Sentient definition: Able to precieve or feel things.
